Shipping Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

At Pure Plucks, we take pride in delivering farm-fresh produce directly to your doorstep. As a farm-to-consumer platform, our shipping practices are thoughtfully designed around natural harvesting cycles and logistical efficiency to ensure the freshest experience possible.

1. Shipping Timeline

Orders are typically shipped within 1 to 7 business days from the date of confirmation. Delivery timelines vary based on your geographical location, availability of fresh harvest, and logistics partner capacity.

2. Fresh Harvest Fulfillment

We align our shipping schedules with harvesting patterns. Some products are dispatched immediately, while others may require waiting until the next fresh harvest. This model guarantees better quality and nutritional value for our customers.

3. Shipping Partners

We collaborate with trusted third-party courier and logistics providers to manage nationwide delivery. While we monitor shipping closely, actual delivery timelines may vary due to external operational factors.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees may vary depending on your location, order value, and delivery method selected. All applicable charges will be clearly visible at checkout before order placement. Occasionally, free shipping may be offered under promotional campaigns.

5. Delivery Areas

We deliver to most serviceable PIN codes in India. If your location is currently unserviceable, you will be notified during checkout. We’re actively expanding our delivery network to reach more areas.

6. Order Processing and Handling

  • Orders are processed Monday to Saturday (excluding public holidays).
  • Processing may be delayed during periods of high demand, extreme weather, or limited harvest.
  • All perishable items are packed in protective, hygienic packaging suitable for transit.

7. Address Accuracy

Please ensure all shipping details (name, address, phone number, PIN code) are accurate and complete. Incorrect or incomplete addresses may cause delivery failure or delays. We are not liable for non-delivery in such cases.

8. Delivery Attempts & Failure

Our logistics partners typically attempt delivery up to 2 times. If the recipient is unavailable or the delivery fails after these attempts, the order may be canceled and not eligible for refund due to the perishable nature of the products.

9. Tracking and Notifications

Once your order is shipped, tracking details will be shared via email/SMS (if applicable). You can monitor the status of your shipment until delivery.

10. Delays and External Factors

  • Natural calamities, political disruptions, strikes, or logistical breakdowns may cause delays beyond our control.
  • In such cases, we strive to inform customers promptly and resolve issues at the earliest.

11. International Shipping

Currently, we do not offer international shipping. Our services are limited to domestic delivery within India.

12. Support and Escalation

If you experience shipping issues or delays beyond the stated window, please contact our support team at pureplucks@gmail.com. Include your order number and relevant details to help us assist you faster.

13. Policy Updates

Pure Plucks reserves the right to amend this Shipping Policy at any time. Any changes will be updated on this page with a revised effective date.
