Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

Welcome to Pure Plucks, an eCommerce platform operated by RBO Ventures. As farmers and direct producers, we sell our own fresh, high-quality agricultural produce through this digital storefront. Please note that RBO Ventures is registered as a digital/eCommerce entity and not as a food or FMCG brand.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone, address) during sign-up and checkout.
  • Payment details via third-party secure gateways (we do not store card data).
  • Browser/device data and cookies to improve your experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To personalize your shopping experience.
  • To send order updates and promotional offers (only with consent).
  • To improve website performance and customer service.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, firewalls, and authentication tools to protect your data. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Product Classification & Transparency

We grow and supply our own produce. Not all items are organically certified. Certified organic items are clearly labeled. We maintain full transparency in labeling and sourcing.

5. Returns and Refunds

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns. Refunds are considered only after case-by-case evaluation by our Help Center team. Your satisfaction is important, and genuine issues will be resolved responsibly.

6. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • Information is shared only with trusted logistics or payment providers strictly to fulfill your order.

7. Your Rights

  • You can request, update, or delete your personal data anytime.
  • You can opt-out of marketing emails from your dashboard or by contacting support.

8. Third-Party Services

We may link to external sites or services. Please review their privacy policies independently.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact our support at: pureplucks@gmail.com.
