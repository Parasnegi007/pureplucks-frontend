Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

At Pure Plucks, operated by RBO Ventures, we grow and deliver fresh farm produce directly to your doorstep. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we follow a strict return and cancellation policy as outlined below:

1. No Returns

We do not accept returns on any product once delivered, as our items are perishable and intended for immediate consumption. Please ensure someone is available to receive the order at the scheduled delivery time.

2. No Cancellations

Orders once placed cannot be cancelled or modified, as we begin processing and harvesting immediately to ensure freshness and timely delivery.

3. Refunds (Conditional)

  • Refunds are issued only in genuine cases such as wrong product delivery, damaged items, or complete non-delivery.
  • Customers must contact our Help Center within 24 hours of delivery with proper photographic evidence and order details.
  • After reviewing the issue, our support team may offer a full or partial refund based on case severity and verification.

4. Late or Missing Refunds

  • Once approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • If the refund hasn’t been received within the timeframe, please first check with your bank, then contact us at pureplucks@gmail.com.

5. Quality Commitment

We stand by the quality of our produce. Each order is hand-picked and packed fresh. If you’re dissatisfied with any part of your experience, we encourage you to contact us for resolution—we’re here to help.

6. Contact

For any refund-related queries, reach out to us at pureplucks@gmail.com.
